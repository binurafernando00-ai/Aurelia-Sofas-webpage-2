A.N.Sofa (Aurelia Sofas) began with a simple belief: furniture shouldn't just fill a space; it should define it. Rooted in Panadura, our workshop is a place where traditional Sri Lankan craftsmanship meets modern aesthetic sensibilities.

From the selection of seasoned timber to the final hand-stitched hem, we pour our heart into creating pieces that are as durable as they are beautiful.
